01 — who I am

About

I'm Khaled Yasser, a full-stack web developer and Artificial Intelligence student at the Faculty of Engineering, Kafrelsheikh University (Class of 2029). I like building things end to end — designing the interface in Figma, wiring it up with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, then giving it a real backend with PHP, Laravel and MySQL so it actually does something.

I care about interfaces that feel considered — good spacing, clear hierarchy, and small details that make a page feel finished rather than assembled. Studying AI alongside web development also means I'm always looking at how these tools can fit into the way I build, not just what I build.

03 — selected work

Projects

Front-End · Travel & Tourism

Book Flights

A front-end travel & tourism web app built for browsing trips and promotional offers. Destinations are organized into clear categories (cities & countries, nature, mountains, river trips), backed by a multi-condition filter bar for budget, country, trip length and purpose. Discounted prices are highlighted with a struck-through old price, and a testimonials section with star ratings adds social proof. The whole interface leans on a large hero image up top and a glassmorphic login card for a modern, polished feel.
